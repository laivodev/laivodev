More Than a Clinic — A Vision

Paws & Claws Veterinary is a forward-thinking concept practice that redefines preventive care. Founded by integrative medicine advocates, we bridge advanced diagnostics with low-stress handling. Our mission is to support the human-animal bond through transparency, accessibility, and continuous education.

We are a concept model for next-generation veterinary medicine: paperless workflows, telemedicine consultations, and an eco-conscious facility design. Every pet receives personalized attention, whether it’s a routine vaccine or complex surgery.
