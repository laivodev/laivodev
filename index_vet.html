About Paws & Claws

Compassion, excellence, and innovation since 2012.

A Hospital Built on Love

Founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, Paws & Claws Veterinary has grown into a full-service animal hospital serving thousands of happy pets. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality medicine with a gentle touch.

We believe in preventive care, client education, and building lasting relationships with both pets and their families.
