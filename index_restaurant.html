A Legacy of Flavor & Warmth

Since 2012, Golden Spoon has been a destination for those who appreciate the finer side of dining. Our philosophy is simple: honor fresh ingredients, celebrate culinary craftsmanship, and create unforgettable experiences.

Led by Chef Michael Romano, our kitchen blends contemporary techniques with classic European influences. Every plate tells a story of passion, precision, and hospitality.

We are proud to be recognized with the Excellence in Gastronomy Award and featured in "Top 50 Restaurants" for three consecutive years.

— Chef Michael Romano & Team
